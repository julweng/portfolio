About

Hi, I'm Julia. I am a full stack web developer based in Bellevue, WA.

Web development is my passion. I enjoy problem solving - the confusion, frustration, and then...the 'Eureka!' moment. I love learning about writing efficient code, awesome libraries, tools, and frameworks.

Before getting into programming, I was a biomedical researcher specialized in decoding the secret of DNA. Now, I am using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to code ideas to life. Making the front and back ends communicate nicely with each other is extremely fun and exciting!
